# Chapter 1 — Picking a Bug Bounty Program (Elite Notes)

**Key Strategy:** Don’t hunt everywhere. Choose programs smartly.

## What to Look For
- Clear, detailed scope (domains, assets, exclusions).
- Good communication and responsive triage teams.
- Programs that value beginners (low noise, simple scope).
- Realistic payouts — not necessarily the highest, but consistent.

## Good Signs
- Public program with steady reports.
- Programs with recent activity and fast response times.
- Programs that welcome researchers with detailed rules.

## Bad Signs
- Extremely large scope with thousands of hunters.
- No triager communication.
- No accepted reports for months.
- Unclear reward structure.

## Deep Evaluation Checklist
- Are there recent accepted reports?
- Are triagers fast?
- How clear is the scope?
- What technologies are used?
- Are APIs included?

## Action Recommendation
Pick **ONE program** and hunt it continuously for **30 days**.
